# SHORT VIDEO SCRIPT — Pulse

**Format:** 30-second screen recording with voiceover
**Platform:** TikTok, Reels, YouTube Shorts

---

## Hook (0–5s)

**Screen:** Pulse dashboard loads on a dark background — streak counter visible, today's workout card centered.
**Voiceover:** "Fitness tracker. No login. No account. No data in the cloud. Let me show you what it does."

---

## Show (5–20s)

**Screen (5–9s):** Click into the Workout Tracker. Hit "Build Workout." Type "bench" in the exercise search — Bench Press appears instantly. Add it. Add two more exercises in quick taps.
**Voiceover:** "Build a workout in seconds from 70 exercises—"

**Screen (9–13s):** Hit "Start Session." The timer starts running. Log a set — weight and reps entered, checkmark tapped. A PR badge flashes on screen.
**Voiceover:** "— run it live with a session timer. PR detected automatically."

**Screen (13–16s):** Navigate to Progress. Body weight chart is visible. Scroll to the consistency heatmap — green squares fill in.
**Voiceover:** "Track body weight, see your consistency—"

**Screen (16–20s):** Click Goals. Show one strength goal at 78% with a clean progress bar. Tap back to the dashboard. Weekly stats summary visible.
**Voiceover:** "— chase real goals with live progress."

---

## Close (20–30s)

**Screen:** Settings panel opens — toggle from dark to light mode (smooth transition). Switch units from kg to lb. Tap Export — JSON file download triggers.
**Voiceover:** "Dark mode, custom units, JSON export. Everything stays on your device — always."
**Text overlay (last 4s):** `Pulse — Open Source · React 19 · No Account Needed`
**Voiceover:** "Free. Open source. Link in bio."
